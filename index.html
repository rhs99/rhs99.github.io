Undergrad Thesis

Topic: Cancer Genomic Analysis of non-coding Regions Bioinformatics | Statistics | Machine Learning | Python

Supervised by
  1. Dr. Atif Rahman
  2. Dr. Md Abul Hassan Samee

We tried to identify significant mutations in non-coding regions through statistical analysis. We used PCAWG cancer data and ATAC-seq peak-calls to get the mutually exclusive gene peak pairs based on p-values. We used WeSME for calculating the p-values. We used WeSME as it performed reasonably well considering the size of our dataset. We have also tried to develop a new method to find mutual exclusivity and co-occurrence using logistic regression and likelihood ratio test.
